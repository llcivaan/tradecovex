Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · IVAAN INC, Toronto, Ontario, Canada

1. Free Trial

All Tradecovex plans include a 7-day free trial with no credit card required. You will not be charged anything during the trial period. You may cancel at any time during the trial with no obligation.

2. Monthly Subscriptions

If you are on a monthly subscription and are not satisfied within the first 7 days of your first paid billing cycle, contact us at support@tradecovex.com and we will issue a full refund of that month's payment. After the first 7 days of a paid billing period, refunds are not available for that period. Cancellation stops future charges immediately.

3. Annual Subscriptions

Annual plans are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of the annual billing date if you are not satisfied. After 14 days, annual subscriptions are non-refundable for the remainder of the annual term. To request a refund on an annual plan, contact support@tradecovex.com with your order number.

4. How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard or by emailing support@tradecovex.com. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You retain full access until the end of the period you have paid for.

5. Exceptions — No Refund Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following circumstances:

  • You were banned or suspended from Tradecovex for violating our Terms of Service.
  • You failed a prop firm challenge while using Tradecovex. Trading outcomes are entirely your responsibility.
  • Your prop firm disqualified your account for use of a trade copier. You are responsible for ensuring Tradecovex complies with your prop firm rules before use.
  • The refund request is made outside the eligible window stated above.

6. Processing

Refunds are processed through Lemon Squeezy, our payment processor and Merchant of Record. Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method within 5–10 business days depending on your bank or card issuer.

7. Contact

For all refund requests: support@tradecovex.com
Please include your order number and the email address used to purchase. We respond within 1 business day.
