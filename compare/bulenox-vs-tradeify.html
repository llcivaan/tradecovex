Bulenox vs Tradeify — Budget Prop Firm Showdown 2026

📅 Updated April 2026 ⏱ 12 min read ✍ Tradecovex Team
Quick Answer

Bulenox is the cheapest evaluation option with aggressive promotional pricing ($30–$50 for a 50K eval), 100% profit on the first $10K, weekly payouts, and explicit copier/bot permission. Tradeify offers $0 activation fees, EOD drawdown on all plans, and three account families (Select, Growth, Lightning). Bulenox wins on raw evaluation price and copier friendliness. Tradeify wins on activation cost and funded-phase flexibility. Both are newer firms competing in the budget tier.

02Evaluation pricing

Bulenox has the cheapest evaluations in the industry when promotional pricing is active. A 50K account can drop to $30–$50/month. Tradeify's cheapest path is the Growth evaluation at roughly $97/month, or Select at $159/month.

The flip: Bulenox charges one-time activation fees of $143–$898 depending on account size and drawdown type. Tradeify charges $0 activation. For a 50K account, Bulenox's total cost is lower with promotional eval pricing. For 100K and 150K accounts, Tradeify's $0 activation can offset the higher eval cost.

03Drawdown comparison

Bulenox gives you a choice: intraday trailing or EOD drawdown at purchase. The decision is locked in permanently. Bulenox also gives $2,500 drawdown on a 50K account. Tradeify uses EOD trailing across all plans with $2,000 drawdown on a 50K account. Bulenox gives you more room ($500 more) and more choice.

04Copier and automation rules

Bulenox is the most copier-friendly firm in the industry. They explicitly permit copiers, bots, algorithms, and automated trading in their rules. Tradeify allows copiers for standard use but prohibits HFT bots and requires EA owners to prove ownership. For copier traders, Bulenox's blanket permission is cleaner.

05Funded-phase flexibility

Tradeify Select Flex has no consistency rule once funded. Bulenox enforces a 40% consistency rule throughout. If you have a trading style that produces lumpy profits (a few big days carrying the month), Tradeify's Flex path is significantly more forgiving in the funded phase.

Bulenox wins on evaluation price and copier rules. Tradeify wins on activation cost and funded-phase consistency flexibility. Choose based on which phase matters more to you.

08Final verdict

Bulenox is the cheapest evaluation and the most bot-friendly firm. Tradeify is the zero-activation, zero-consistency funded option with more account variety. Both are strong budget choices. Running both with a trade copier is a common strategy — Bulenox's weekly payouts and Tradeify's funded-phase flexibility complement each other.
